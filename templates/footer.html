Ukraine Needs You

Ukraine needs you is a non-profit organizatiom formed by a group of citizens from eastern part of Ukraine to seek for outside support for our people who are suffering due to the war between Russia and UKraine. There is nothing we can do without your help. God bless you as you donate to save children from freezing to death or dying of hunger.
